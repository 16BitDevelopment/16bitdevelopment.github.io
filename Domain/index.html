Step 1

Go to the website namecheap.com and type in your domain name. Press search and follow the prompts to buy your domain name.

Step 2

After buying your domain name, go back to namecheap.com and locate to "Account", then "Dashboard".

Step 3

Click on "Domains List".

Step 4

Find your domain name, then click "Manage".

Step 5

Click "Advanced DNS".

Step 6

Click "Add New Record" and enter the information: "A record", "@", [IP address], "20 min".

Do this 4 times with the IP address being:

  • 185.199.108.153
  • 185.199.109.153
  • 185.199.110.153
  • 185.199.111.153

Click "Save All Changes". (Don't worry if the old records disappear)

Step 7

Click "Add New Record" and enter the information:

"CNAME Record", "www", 16bitdevelopment.github.io/[your website name].github.io, "20 min".

For example, if your website name was "mywebsite", you would put in "16bitdevelopment.github.io/mywebsite.github.io"

Finally, click "Save All Changes".

Congrats!

You have finished all of the steps!
